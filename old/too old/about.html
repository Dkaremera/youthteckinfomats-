We Are Here To Take The Youth To The Next Level.

Rwanda Youth Tech Informants (RYTI) is a non-profit organization founded to bridge the digital divide in rural Rwanda by equipping high school graduates with skills and knowledge to access digital services provided by the government of Rwanda and other necessary information needed for a smooth integration of Rwanda's rural area residents in a modern world, a world of technology.

Commutted To Provide Access To Rural Youth .

RYTI is an initiative focused on bridging the digital divide in rural Rwanda. We believe that everyone deserves access to the digital tools and resources necessary for success in today's world. Our project is aimed at empowering high school graduates in rural areas with the skills and knowledge they need to access government online services, use computer devices, and the internet. By doing so, we hope to create a more equitable and connected Rwanda.
